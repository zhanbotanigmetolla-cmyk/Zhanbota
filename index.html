Privacy Policy

turnikmen_challenge_bot / Turnikmen

Effective date: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Bot collects, how it is used, and when it may be shared.

By starting or continuing to use this Bot, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of your data as described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Bot or delete your account through the Bot's settings.

1. Data We Collect

When you use the Bot, we may collect and store:

  • Your Telegram user ID
  • Your Telegram username
  • Your first name or display name
  • Your selected language
  • Your join date
  • Your body weight
  • Your training base and program day
  • Your reminder time
  • Your XP, level, streak, freeze tokens, personal record, and last workout date
  • Your workout history, including date, planned reps, completed reps, sets, RPE, day type, extra activity, extra activity duration, and workout notes
  • Bug reports you send through the Bot
  • Temporary conversation state needed to complete registration, training, settings, and similar flows
  • Technical logs needed to operate, secure, and debug the Bot, including message text, callback data, user ID, and error details

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To register and identify your account inside the Bot
  • To generate and personalize your training plan
  • To save your workout history and statistics
  • To calculate streaks, XP, levels, and personal records
  • To send workout reminders and weekly summaries
  • To provide social features such as the participants list, leaderboard, and motivational poke messages
  • To process bug reports and fix problems
  • To monitor performance, diagnose errors, and maintain the Bot

3. Visibility to Other Users

The Bot includes social features. Because of that, limited data about your activity may be visible to other Bot users:

  • Your display name may be shown in the participants list and leaderboard
  • Your current streak and limited workout progress may be shown in the participants list and leaderboard
  • Your Telegram username, if available, may be shown in social interaction buttons inside the Bot
  • When a new account is created, the Bot may announce the new participant's name to existing users
  • Linked users may receive a message when you finish a workout
  • Other users may be able to send you a motivational poke through the Bot

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot works through Telegram, so your use of the Bot is also subject to Telegram's own privacy practices.

Turnikmen AI — the built-in AI chat feature — is powered by Google Gemini 3 Flash via the Google AI API. When you send a message in the AI chat, the following data is transmitted to Google's servers to generate a response:

  • Your display name
  • Your current rank and XP
  • Your streak count and freeze token balance
  • Your daily base pullup value and body weight
  • Your personal record
  • A summary of your last 14 workouts (date, planned reps, completed reps, RPE, day type)
  • The text of your message and the conversation history for the current chat session (up to 10 exchanges)

This data is sent to Google solely to produce the AI response and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. The Turnikmen AI feature is optional — you can use the Bot fully without ever opening it.

5. Admin Access

The Bot administrator may receive error alerts, technical diagnostics, bug reports, and basic operational summaries such as total user counts and workout counts. In some cases, error diagnostics may include message text or button data related to the failed action.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored until you delete your account, your account is automatically removed after extended inactivity, or your account is removed after blocking the Bot and remaining inactive.

  • Inactive accounts may be warned after 27 days without training
  • Inactive accounts may be deleted after 30 days without training
  • If the Bot cannot deliver a reminder because it was blocked, and the account has also been inactive for 7 or more days, the account may be removed

7. Your Choices

  • You can update settings such as language, weight, base value, and reminder time
  • You can choose whether to use the AI advice feature
  • You can choose whether to send a bug report
  • You can delete your account and erase stored Bot data through the Bot's settings

8. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing on their own.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest published version will apply from its stated effective date. Your continued use of the Bot after a new version is published constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.

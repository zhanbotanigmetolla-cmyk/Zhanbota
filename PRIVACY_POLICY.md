# Privacy Policy for Pullup Pro / Turnikmen Telegram Bot

Effective date: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Pullup Pro / Turnikmen Telegram bot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and when it may be shared.

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use the Bot, we may collect and store:

- Your Telegram user ID
- Your Telegram username
- Your first name or display name
- Your selected language
- Your join date
- Your body weight
- Your training base and program day
- Your reminder time
- Your XP, level, streak, freeze tokens, personal record, and last workout date
- Your workout history, including date, planned reps, completed reps, sets, RPE, day type, extra activity, extra activity duration, and workout notes
- Bug reports you send through the Bot
- Temporary conversation state needed to complete registration, training, settings, and similar flows
- Technical logs needed to operate, secure, and debug the Bot, including message text, callback data, user ID, and error details

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- register and identify your account inside the Bot
- generate and personalize your training plan
- save your workout history and statistics
- calculate streaks, XP, levels, and personal records
- send workout reminders and weekly summaries
- provide social features such as the participants list, leaderboard, and motivational poke messages
- process bug reports and fix problems
- monitor performance, diagnose errors, and maintain the Bot

## 3. When Your Data May Be Visible to Other Users

The Bot includes social features. Because of that, limited data about your activity may be visible to other Bot users:

- your display name may be shown in the participants list and leaderboard
- your current streak and limited workout progress may be shown in the participants list and leaderboard
- your Telegram username, if available, may be shown in social interaction buttons inside the Bot
- when a new account is created, the Bot may announce the new participant's name to existing users
- if social notifications are enabled for your account, linked users may receive a message when you finish a workout
- other users may be able to send you a motivational "poke" through the Bot

If you do not want this data to remain in the Bot, you can delete your account at any time.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Bot works through Telegram, so your use of the Bot is also subject to Telegram's own privacy practices.

If you use the AI advice feature, limited training data is sent to Groq's API to generate a response. This may include your display name, weight, streak, level, base training value, current program day, upcoming training day type, and a short summary of your recent workouts. The AI feature is optional.

## 5. Admin Access

The Bot administrator may receive:

- error alerts and technical diagnostics
- bug reports you submit
- basic operational summaries, such as total user counts and workout counts

In some cases, error diagnostics may include message text or button data related to the failed action.

This access is used only to operate, support, and improve the Bot.

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is stored until one of the following happens:

- you delete your account through the Bot
- your account is automatically removed after extended inactivity
- your account is removed after blocking the Bot and remaining inactive

At the time of this policy:

- inactive accounts may be warned after 27 days without training
- inactive accounts may be deleted after 30 days without training
- if the Bot cannot deliver a reminder because it was blocked, and the account has also been inactive for 7 or more days, the account may be removed

Temporary conversation state may remain stored until the flow is completed, cancelled, or cleared by the Bot.

Technical logs may be retained for troubleshooting and security as reasonably necessary.

## 7. Your Choices

You can:

- update certain settings such as language, weight, base value, and reminder time
- choose whether to use the AI advice feature
- choose whether to send a bug report
- delete your account and erase stored Bot data through the Bot's settings

## 8. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. By using the Bot, you understand and accept this risk.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing on their own.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest published version will apply from its stated effective date.
